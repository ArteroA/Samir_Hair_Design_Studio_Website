Behind the Chair

Samir

Owner & stylist
One chair • One client at a time

Samir works with a simple principle — great hair isn’t rushed, it’s built with intention.

His focus is clean execution, honest advice, and results that grow out properly over time, not just how they look on day one.

Every appointment is handled personally. Whether it’s a quick cleanup or a full transformation, the work in his cozy salon gets his full attention from start to finish. His European training gives him a strong foundation.

Clients often describe him as someone who remembers their hair, their patterns, and how they like to feel when they leave the chair, not just the service they booked.

You're likely to find Samir mid-foils, fully locked into his craft, with something on in the background — coffee and water always on offer.
